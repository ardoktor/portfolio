A Bit About How I Work

I'm Arda -- a product-focused software engineer who builds AI-assisted systems around real operational problems.

My background spans network infrastructure and backend engineering, but my core strength lies in designing intelligent workflows: systems that combine LLMs, retrieval, and automation to support real users in healthcare, education, and career development. I don't build demos -- I build products. From clinical knowledge systems (ConsulDent), to long-term mentorship journeys (MentAI), to resume and interview gap analysis (mulakat.pro), my work consistently revolves around the same question:

"How can AI reduce complexity and guide better decisions?"

I'm deeply focused on retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), agent-based architectures, and structured AI outputs. I care about correctness, traceability, and usability -- not just model outputs, but how those outputs fit into real workflows.

Every project I build is treated as a potential product. That means owning the full lifecycle: system design, backend logic, deployment, iteration, and continuous improvement based on user feedback.
